The public Augmented Reality kinectome, or pARk, is a framework for 5G immersive internets.

In a pARk, you can interact with the world as you are meant to -- with your hands and natural senses -- while still possessing the benefits of digital impressions, search, and machine processing.

We are no longer constrained to abstractions like the Document Object Model. Instead, one can make digital services and experiences part-and-parcel of the natural flow of your every day life.

Each pARk is managed by a distributed autonomous organization, or DAO, known as a Community. Communities act as decentralized service providers and expose pARk access via the I See Dead People paradigm.
